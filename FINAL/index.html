SURF OASIS

Professional surf lessons that will have you riding waves after just one session!

Water Safety

Our students will learn about wave dynamics, ocean safety and awareness, how to deal with rip-currents, popping up on a surf board, handling the board in the water, turning, wave selection, positioning and maneuvering, surfing etiquette, and much, much more.

Dates & Location

Offered at our Santa Monica/Venice Beach and Manhattan Beach/El Porto location, April 1 to April 19, Monday through Friday

Beach Activities

Other beach activities we offer are: paddling, boogie-boarding, swimming, body-surfing, running, sand sculpture, and beach games such as Frisbee, Nerf touch-football, beach volleyball, dodge-ball and beach soccer. We also have break times to dry off, rehydrate and relax or play.
